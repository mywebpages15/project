Messi's description

Lionel Messi is an Argentine footballer widely considered one of the greatest football players of all time. He was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina.

Messi is primarily known for his outstanding career at FC Barcelona, where he spent the majority of his professional career.
